About Us

Who We Are

5 Star Development Group, LLC is a professional, full service real estate solutions firm that buys and sells properties throughout the greater Bay area. We specialize in buying distressed homes at a significant discount, and renovate and resell them to retail home buyers and landlords. Founded in 2020 by a group of investors, 5 Star Development Group is excited to be part of the area’s renaissance and we aspire to continue contributing to the economic rejuvenation of the Bay Area and its neighborhoods.
